Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How far in advance should I book?

A: We recommend booking at least 3-5 hours in advance, especially during peak moving weekends. However, feel free to contact us last minute, and we'll do our best to accommodate you!

Q: What forms of payment do you accept?

A: We accept mobile money (MTN & Vodafone) and cash for maximum convenience.

Q: What is your pricing?

A: Our pricing is simple and based on the vehicle and service. Please see our Services section above for details.

Q: Are you insured?

A: While we take the utmost care with every item we move, we are a student-run service. We recommend students ensure their own valuable items. Our team is trained to handle all belongings with care.

Q: What hostels do you serve?

A: We serve all major hostels around Presbyterian University, including Obenewah, Franbenny, Cementary, Ebenezer, and the official university hostels.
